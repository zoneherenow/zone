zone 境

Another world is here now


zone (境) is a cross-disciplinary theatre company.

We make theatre that is preoccupied with the symbolic, psychic, and unconscious. Our creative process is intuition-led, and moves beyond the logics of siloed disciplines and traditional narrative structures.

We are invested in performances that reverberate past the level of plot—conveying moods, atmospheres, hunches, suspicions, and desires; and enacting temporary environments, relationships, and shared conditions.

Through our work, we aim to conjure, enact, and realize zones where alternative realities and ways of being are possible.


zone (境) is initiated and led by kyatos / Ang Kia Yee. Our logos were co-designed with and realized by Ow Ting Quan.
