friendzones


5 April, 6pm – 10pm
Goodman Arts Centre Blk B Studio
free

tickets

friendzones is a relaxed, introvert-friendly social gathering where we hang out over food, drinks, and slow conversations into the night. We will make it an easy space and look out for quieter folks, while giving plenty of room for people to meet and connect at their own pace.

We'll start at a GAC studio for a round of food, then migrate to a second venue for drinks. Feel free to arrive whenever works for you, and leave whenever you need to. We'll cover the cost of the first round of food, but drinks and additional nibbles are on your own tab!

Just a few gentle asks: be respectful of people’s energy and personal space and take care of your own boundaries too. If anything unpleasant or harmful happens, please let one of us know.


artist

zone 境



zone 境 is a cross-disciplinary theatre company which gathers at the borders where language fails, to do the impossible work of expressing the unspeakable and unnameable. By conjuring transient zones where alternative realities and ways of being are possible, we remind ourselves of the possibilities of the impossible.
